# limma-eBayes cross-cohort panel (WG)

Generated by `scripts/limma_cross_cohort_panel.py`. Aggregates the
per-cohort limma-candidate ranking TSVs against the V2.5 gating
TSVs to produce a 3-cohort × 3-axis comparison at whole-genome
scale. Both axes are ranked on the **same frozen WG catalog**
(HM450: SHA256 `d20661c5…`; native EPIC v2: `39df8f0f…`); limma
t-stats come from the sample-level β matrix joined to the catalog
via the V2.5 scored JSONL's `observation.probe_id` field
(probe-level limma first, then candidate-mapped — per §7 Methods).
