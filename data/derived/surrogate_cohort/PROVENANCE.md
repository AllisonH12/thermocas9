# Surrogate matched cell-line cohort

**NOT** the Roth et al. Nature 2026 samples — their cited GEO accession (GSE32256) resolves to unrelated Paramecium data and has been flagged to the authors.

This cohort is built from **GSE77348** (Ostler et al., 2016, "DNMT3B gene isoforms revealed specific methylation..."), HM450 platform:
  - MCF-7  untreated replicates: 3 samples
  - MCF-10A untreated replicates: 3 samples

Used as a **structural surrogate** to test whether V2 p_therapeutic_selectivity recovers under a cell-line-style matched comparator where the normal actually methylates target promoters. Not a substitute for the Roth validation data; do not publish benchmark numbers without this provenance caveat.
